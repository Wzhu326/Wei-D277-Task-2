About New York State

One of the 13 original colonies and states, New York State is located in the northeastern United States, on the east coast. Cities are spread across a diverse geography that includes mountains, lakes, rivers, and coastal areas. The State is home to significant areas such as the Hudson River, the Catskills Mountains, The Adirondack Mountains, and the Greak Lakes.

As of 2023, about 19.77 million people reside in New York State. The capital city of New York State is Albany, with the largest city being New York City. Every city in New York State is rich with history and unique characteristics that make each one stand out and worth visiting.

Our site plans to introduce and help you learn about each and every one of the amazing cities for your next visit!
